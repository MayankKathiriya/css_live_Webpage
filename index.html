Good Health Can't Wait

Our credo captures the essence of who we are and what we believe in. We see medicines as not just molecules, but also as a means to help people lead healthy lives.

We believe that good health must be delivered speedily to as many patients as possible. We target therapy areas with unmet patient needs and aim to create accessible and affordable solutions.

Manufacturing :

DR.REDDY'S has 21 state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in various areas like Paonta Sahib, Himachal Pradesh, Sikkim, Vizag & Rajasthan. All manufacturing facilities are designed as per cGMP and FDA guidelines. Being one of the fastest-growing pharmaceutical companies in India , we have always invested in building modern manufacturing facilities to increase our production capacities and meet the growing demands of society.

Over the past 37 years, talented and capable people have powered Dr. Reddy's growth and built our reputation as a world-class pharmaceutical company. Because when people with diverse skills are bound together by a common purpose and values, they can make magic.
